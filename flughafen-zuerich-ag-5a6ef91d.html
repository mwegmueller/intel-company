Regulatory Framework

EASA AI Trustworthiness (NPA 2025-07)

European Union Aviation Safety Agency framework for AI certification in aviation operations. Defines trustworthiness standards for autonomous systems and AI-assisted decision making in airport operations.

EU AI Act - High-Risk Classification

Autonomous vehicles, biometric systems, and critical infrastructure AI require extensive compliance documentation, impact assessments, and human oversight mechanisms.

Swiss FOCA Regulations

Federal Office of Civil Aviation requirements for AI systems in flight operations, ground handling, and air traffic management at Swiss airports.

Airport Carbon Accreditation Level 4

Sustainability certification requiring AI-powered carbon tracking, optimization of airport operations, and renewable energy integration roadmaps.

Noise Regulations & Smart Operations

ICAO standards for noise management. AI-powered ground operations and routing optimization supporting compliance with increasingly strict noise limits.
